These are an example of projects I have designed using both Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The first image was done for my Design Process Practice module. The brief was to design a poster for the lecture theatre in the college without the use of words.

The stamp was designed for the AHEAD conference, celebrating 100 years of education.

In my spare time I regulary work with stencils. I design the stencils in Photoshop and Illustrator, then cut them out on perspex. I use spray paint to build the layers. The Pulp Fiction and Star Trek stencils are based on images by Noma Bar, a famous graphic designer. The geisha above is my own design and is comprised of nine layers.
